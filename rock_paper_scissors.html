How to play Rock Paper Scissors

THIS IS A TWO PLAYER GAME.

  1. Find another player to play with
  2. Count down from 3. When the timer hits zero, show your pick of rock, paper or scissors.
  3. If you and your opponent choose the same thing, redo step 2. Otherwise, proceed onto the next step.
  4. Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock, scissors beats paper. Based on those rules, determine who wins.
